The Homeland
  • U.S. territory — ~56,000 residents
  • Rich fa'asamoa cultural foundation
  • Highest military service rate per capita
  • Limited federal program access
  • Emerging entrepreneurial base
  • Youth athletics pipeline

Samoan Americans
The Diaspora
  • 55,000+ across the U.S. mainland
  • California, Hawaii, Utah as major hubs
  • Growing professional class
  • High rate of sports excellence
  • Cultural identity navigation
  • Capital & network access
The Pacific Islander Ecosystem creates infrastructure for mutual uplift — channeling mainland networks, resources, and professional development back to American Samoa while honoring the homeland's cultural wisdom that sustains diaspora identity.

Vā
/ vā /  ·  Samoan

The sacred, relational space between people. In Pacific Islander philosophy, the vā is not empty space but a living connection — the relationship itself, honoured and sustained by both parties.

Rooted in fa'asamoa — the Samoan way of life and its obligations of mutual care and community.

Who joins the Vā?
  • Pacific Islander professionals and entrepreneurs
  • Current and former Pacific Islander athletes
  • Students and emerging community leaders
  • Educators, creatives, and advocates
  • American Samoans on island and stateside
  • Allies, partners, and community champions
