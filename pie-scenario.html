A composite scenario
Sina Faleolo
17 · Libero · Pago Pago, American Samoa
“They want me to leave. They also don’t want me to leave. Both are love. — PIE is the first place where both can be true at once.”
Background
First in her family to be recruited off-island. Eldest daughter.
The Situation
Three D1 offers. Six thousand miles between the kitchen table and a dorm room she has never seen.
The Gap
No Pasifika contact at any program. No one who has made this choice and can speak both languages — literally and culturally.
